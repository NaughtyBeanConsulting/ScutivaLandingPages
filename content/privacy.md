This privacy policy explains how the Scutiva marketing site handles information. Scutiva is an open-source, self-hosted project, and this site is intended primarily to provide project information, repository links, and contact details.

## Website data

This site may collect limited technical data that is normally available to web infrastructure, such as IP address, browser details, request timing, and standard server logs. This data is used for basic site operation, security, and debugging.

## Contact and correspondence

If you contact us by email at [hello@scutiva.com](mailto:hello@scutiva.com), we may retain your message and contact details for the purpose of responding to your inquiry, maintaining project communications, or handling open-source collaboration discussions.

## Donations and payment providers

If you choose to use a donation link from this site, payment processing will be handled by a third-party payment provider. We do not claim to control that provider’s data handling practices. You should review the provider’s privacy policy before submitting payment information.

## Open-source project data

If you interact with the Scutiva GitHub repository, those interactions are governed by GitHub’s own privacy terms and platform policies.

## Self-hosted deployments

The Scutiva software is self-hosted by its users. Data processed inside a self-hosted Scutiva deployment is controlled by the operator running that deployment, not by this marketing site.

## Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to [hello@scutiva.com](mailto:hello@scutiva.com).
